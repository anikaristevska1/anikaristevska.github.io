About me

Hello! I am Anika Ristevska. I am 19 years old and I was born and live in the capital of the Republic of Macedonia, Skopje.

Born on the first of September, I carry a spirit of diligence, curiosity and a thirst for knowledge. With a modest upbringing in this vibrant city, I flourished amidst the cultural mosaic that defines its identity. My educational journey began at Panayot Ginovski Primary School. I began secondary education at Orce Nikolov, where I honed my academic acumen and nurtured my passion for technology.

Those years were a stepping stone, shaping my path to a future fueled by innovation and endless possibilities. Now, as a student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering, I am embarking on a deep exploration of the digital realm. With every line of code I write and every algorithm I unravel, I dive deeper into the complexities of this ever-evolving field. My mind, brimming with curiosity, thirsts for knowledge and skills that will shape the future of technology.
